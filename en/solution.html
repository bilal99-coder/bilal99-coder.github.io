How do we respond to the challenges?

ScanPlanet makes users more aware of the benefits of being vegan lifestyle. The solution is part of the fight against hunger and the desire for responsibility consumption and production.

Date check

A notification will be sent out a few days before the expiration date on your products. Together we can prevent food waste.

Environmentally friendly alternatives

With our app you can scan products and find environmentally friendly alternatives. You do not even need a barcode!

Vegan recipes

Scan your products and get thousands of vegan recipes in our app. Of course, you do not need a barcode.

To give back

We are a voluntary organization that wants to save the planet as best we can can. All financing and income goes to this purpose. We will donate $ 1 USD to WWF (World Wildlife Fund) for each new user who registers,

We make it easier to choose green

By using our app, you can easily get inspiration for green dishes as well as ease the guilt from all the meat eating and the consumption of the globe. The app publishes regular updates with new research on veganism and related topics. With ScanPlanet gives you a good insight into the vegan lifestyle.
