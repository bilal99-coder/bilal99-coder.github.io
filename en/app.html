Date check

Here you will get an overview of the dates of all items that have been scanned with a barcode. They appear in chronological order based on the expiration date.

This feature will help you use up the foods you have. Reduced food waste provides better climatic conditions. We are working on adding categories and other functionality.

Recipes

The ScanPlanet app contains a memory bank with several thousand tasty, vegan recipes.

You will receive recipe recommendations based on items you have in yours assortment so you can easily avoid unnecessary food waste.

Scan items

The ScanPlanet app can scan all barcodes and put the item directly into the app. Then as a user, you get an automatic overview of the best before date. You will also have access to a variety of recipes associated with the item.

The app uses advanced machine learning to also interpret products without barcode as fruits and vegetables. In addition, you can use the search function at the top.

Research

A number of research articles are available in just one small finger pressure away. Here you can read up on hard facts and Feel free to answer the questions you have.

We will add categorization of articles based on content, length and other factors. Bio about the authors will be available. Book and film recommendations also come here under relevant topic picks.
